Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Landing pages: 1-3 days, Business websites: 3-7 days, Full systems: 7-14 days. We provide regular updates throughout the process.

Do you offer maintenance after delivery?

Yes! We offer 30 days of free maintenance for all projects. After that, we have affordable monthly maintenance plans starting from 1,000 KES/month.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes, for Business Websites and Full Systems, we include a user-friendly admin panel where you can update text, images, and manage your content easily without coding knowledge.

Do you offer hosting and domain services?

Yes! We offer complete hosting solutions starting from 500 KES/month. We can also help you register a domain name (.co.ke, .com, .org, etc.) and set up professional email addresses.
