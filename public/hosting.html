Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common hosting questions

Do you offer free website migration?

Yes! We offer free website migration for all hosting plans. Our team will handle the entire migration process, ensuring zero downtime and seamless transition from your current host.

What is your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all hosting plans. Our servers are monitored 24/7, and we use redundant systems to ensure maximum reliability.

How do I contact support?

We offer 24/7 support via WhatsApp (+254 117 234 064), phone, and email. For Business and Enterprise plans, we provide dedicated WhatsApp support with guaranteed 30-minute response times.

Do you offer Kenyan domain names (.co.ke)?

Yes! We offer all Kenyan domain extensions including .co.ke (800 KES/year), .me.ke (600 KES/year), .or.ke, and .ac.ke. We handle all registration paperwork with KENIC.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and PayPal. Monthly, quarterly, and annual payment options are available with discounts for longer commitments.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade anytime at no extra cost. We'll prorate the difference and migrate your account seamlessly. Downgrades are also possible with 30 days notice.
